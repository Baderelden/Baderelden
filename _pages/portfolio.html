My research interests are in the area Machine Learning (ML), Data Mining, Combinatorial Optimisation and Genetic Programming. I have experience working with industries on adopting and developing applied machine learning methods [example]. I have been involved in several industrial Knowledge Transfer Partnerships.

One of the research areas that I am interested in is developing and applying ML methods for early outcome prediction in real-world applications such Intinsivve Care Units data and Digital Marketing. I am working on developing methods to tackle the problem of class-imbalance in ML data. Also, I have interest in hyper-heuristic and genetic programming for the automatic heuristics design.
